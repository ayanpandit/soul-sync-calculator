What is Numerology in Vedic Tradition? +
Imagine each letter of your name as a magical number! Just like how you count your toys, in Vedic numerology, we count the special energy of each letter. A is like 1 sun, B is like 2 moons, and so on. When we add these numbers together, they tell us about your special qualities, just like how mixing different colors makes a new beautiful color!
How do Planets Influence Us? +
Think of planets as your celestial friends! Each planet has its own personality: - Sun is like a loving father, giving warmth and strength - Moon is like a caring mother, giving love and comfort - Mars is like a brave warrior, giving courage - Mercury is like a wise teacher, giving intelligence - Jupiter is like a kind guru, giving wisdom - Venus is like a beautiful artist, giving love and creativity - Saturn is like a strict but fair judge, teaching important lessons
What is Bhrigu Samhita? +
Long, long ago, there lived a very wise sage named Bhrigu. He was like a magical storyteller who wrote a special book called Bhrigu Samhita. This book is like a giant collection of everyone's life stories, written even before they were born! It's like having a magical diary that knows about your past, present, and future.
What are Planetary Relationships? +
Just like how some of your friends get along better than others, planets also have their best friends and not-so-best friends! - Sun and Moon are best friends (like cookies and milk!) - Jupiter and Mercury love to play together - Venus and Mars have an exciting relationship - Saturn likes to teach important lessons to everyone
How Does Name Energy Work? +
Your name is like a beautiful melody! Each letter sings its own special note, and when we put them all together, they create your life's song. The first letter is extra special - it's like the conductor of your orchestra! That's why in Vedic astrology, we pay special attention to your name's first letter to understand which planet is your special guide.
What are the Different Types of Relationships in Vedic Astrology? +
In Vedic tradition, relationships are like different types of beautiful flowers in a garden: - Soulmate (आत्मीय): The most divine connection, blessed by the gods - Life Partner (जीवनसाथी): A harmonious union of minds and hearts - Divine Friend (दिव्य मित्र): A spiritual connection that transcends ordinary friendship - Karmic Connection (कर्मिक संबंध): A relationship meant to teach important life lessons Each type has its own special purpose in our spiritual journey!

The Legacy of Ancient Indian Astrologers

In the mystical lands of ancient India, great sages and astrologers unlocked the secrets of the cosmos. These wise beings, through deep meditation and divine connection, developed sophisticated systems to understand human relationships and destiny.

The Great Sages

1. Maharishi Bhrigu: Known as the father of predictive astrology, created the legendary Bhrigu Samhita

2. Maharishi Parasara: Developed the fundamental principles of Vedic astrology

3. Varahamihira: Combined Western and Eastern astrological wisdom

Their Major Contributions

  • Development of the Nakshatras system
  • Creation of detailed planetary combinations
  • Understanding of karmic relationships
  • Methods for calculating compatibility

These ancient seers created complex systems that continue to guide millions in their journey of love and life.
